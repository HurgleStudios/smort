About SMORT

SMORT is an emoji based sorting game. Tap / click on a vial to select the top item, and tap another vial to move it. You can only stack objects either in empty vials, or on top of the same type of object. The object is to sort the objects, such that each vial is either empty, or full of the same type of object. The "New Game" button at the bottom left will start a new game, and the "Undo" button at the bottom right will undo the previous move.

In the Options menu (click the gear), you can change the height of the vials, the number of different objects, and whether all the objects are visible, or are hidden until exposed to the top. (You'll see.)
